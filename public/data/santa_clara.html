PHP 2013 will be held at the Santa Clara Convention Center in the heart of Silicon Valley. The Convention Center is a high-tech meeting complex created by the technology businesses that surround it. 

Santa Clara Convention Center
5001 Great America Parkway
Santa Clara, CA 95054
Official Website

The Santa Clara Convention is located adjacent to US Highway 101 and is easily accessible from the San Jose, San Francisco, and Oakland International airports by car and public transportation.

Driving directions to the Santa Clara Convention Center and a Mapquest link are available by clicking here . Parking is free at local parking lots.

Alternative public transportation is available via Amtrak, Caltrain and the Valley Transportation Authority light rail and bus. Please refer to the convention center website for additional information.
