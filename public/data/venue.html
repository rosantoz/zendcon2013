Santa Clara Convention Center  [IMAGE]

PHP 2013 will be held at the Santa Clara Convention Center in the heart of Silicon Valley. The Convention Center is a high-tech meeting complex created by the technology businesses that surround it. 

Santa Clara Convention Center
5001 Great America Parkway
Santa Clara, CA 95054
Official Website

The Santa Clara Convention is located adjacent to US Highway 101 and is easily accessible from the San Jose, San Francisco, and Oakland International airports by car and public transportation.

Driving directions to the Santa Clara Convention Center and a Mapquest link are available by clicking here. Parking is free at local parking lots.

Alternative public transportation is available via Amtrak, Caltrain and the Valley Transportation Authority light rail and bus. Please refer to the convention center website for additional information.

Transportation

San Jose Airport Hotel Shuttle

Visitors seeking San Jose airport shuttle service or transportation to the Hyatt Regency Santa Clara have a variety of options, including taxi service and convenient airport shuttle transportation. The Hyatt concierge can provide additional information about San Jose Airport hotel shuttle and transportation options. Please contact them via email or at (408) 200-1234.


Shuttles can either be arranged over the telephone or can be found waiting in one of two areas noted below. The approximate cost for one-way trip to or from the San Jose Airport is $16.00 - $21.00 per person. Click here to view a list of San Jose Airport shuttle companies.

  • Terminal A - In the Ground Transportation Center located south of the Terminal Garage
  • Terminal C - Outside, same side of street, just south of baggage claim

Airport Shuttle Cost:

San Jose Taxi Service: Taxis are stationed at the San Jose Airport for on-demand service. They are easily accessible from Terminal A and Terminal C.  The approximate cost for a taxi to or from San Jose Airport ranges from $15.00 - $30.00
